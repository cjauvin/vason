Welcome to the VaSOn Annotation Button app!



To begin, please drag this VaSOn Button in your browser's bookmark area or "toolbar".

To use, select some text that you wish to annotate in the page you are currently browser, and click it, and complete the process in the popup window. You can then check in real-time the content of the database, in the grid below.
